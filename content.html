Basic Backup Guide, also known as the (B.B.G)

Use cloud services like Dropbox and Google Drive for small things or group activities!
Keep in mind that both of these servies have paid versions that perform better, so keeping your files across multiple devices might also be a good idea.

Having physical storage like USBs and hard drives is also a great way to keep tons of data at once!
Some 2 terabyte hard drives go for around PHP 1,000, so they're pretty affordable if you need some space or if you want to back up your computer's current state.
Make sure to check the drives for anything suspicious, though.

To back up a computer on Windows 11, head to your control panel, go into System and Security, then look for File History. attach a hard drive/usb, then hit the button and sit back while it backs up your computer!
It'll take a while if you have a ton of stuff on your computer, but it'll let you revert to the saved version of your computer at any time!
